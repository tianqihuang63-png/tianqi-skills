# Safety Boundaries

## Purpose

This skill handles short-term emotional stabilization and action recovery.
It does not handle clinical risk or deep therapy.

## Stop or de-escalate when

The user shows signs like:

- self-harm or suicide references
- extreme hopelessness with safety concern
- inability to function for extended periods
- severe traumatic material needing professional support
- repeated requests for diagnosis or treatment

## What to do

1. Do not continue deep coaching as if this were a normal self-management session.
2. Acknowledge the seriousness.
3. Encourage real-world professional or trusted human support.
4. Prefer immediate, practical safety-oriented guidance over interpretation work.

## Important rule

Do not pretend this skill can safely cover situations outside its boundary.
